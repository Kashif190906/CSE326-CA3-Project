Famous Places to Visit in Berlin

Brandenburg Gate

One of Berlin's most iconic landmarks, symbolizing the reunification of East and West Germany.

The Berlin Wall Memorial

A historic site that offers insights into Berlin's division during the Cold War.

Reichstag Building

The seat of the German Parliament, famous for its glass dome that offers a panoramic view of Berlin.

Berlin Cathedral

The largest church in Berlin, known for its stunning architecture and rich history.

Checkpoint Charlie

The most famous crossing point between East and West Berlin during the Cold War.

Unter den Linden

A grand boulevard that connects Brandenburg Gate to Alexanderplatz, lined with historical landmarks.
