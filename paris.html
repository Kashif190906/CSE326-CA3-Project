Famous Places to Visit in Paris

Eiffel Tower

The iconic symbol of Paris, offering stunning views of the city from its observation decks.

Louvre Museum

Home to thousands of art pieces, including the famous "Mona Lisa" and "Venus de Milo".

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture, famous for its stunning facade and flying buttresses.

Montmartre

The historic hilltop neighborhood known for its art, the Sacré-Cœur Basilica, and stunning views of Paris.

Champs-Élysées

The grand avenue, lined with shops, theaters, and cafes, leading to the Arc de Triomphe.

Musée d'Orsay

A museum housed in a former railway station, featuring art from the Impressionist period and beyond.
