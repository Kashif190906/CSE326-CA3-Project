Famous Places to Visit in London

The British Museum

One of the world’s greatest museums, home to millions of years of history and culture.

The Tower of London

A historic castle that has served as a royal palace, prison, and home to the Crown Jewels.

Big Ben & Houses of Parliament

The iconic clock tower and the seat of the UK government, an absolute must-see landmark in London.

The London Eye

Europe's tallest observation wheel, offering stunning views of London’s skyline.

Hyde Park

A massive green space offering peaceful nature, lakes, and historical monuments in central London.

St. Paul's Cathedral

One of London's most famous churches, renowned for its magnificent dome and stunning interior.
