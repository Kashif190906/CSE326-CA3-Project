Famous Places to Visit in Amsterdam

The Rijksmuseum

One of the most famous museums in the world, home to masterpieces by Dutch artists like Rembrandt and Vermeer.

Anne Frank House

The historic house where Anne Frank hid during World War II, now a museum dedicated to her memory.

Van Gogh Museum

A museum dedicated to the works of Vincent Van Gogh, featuring the world's largest collection of his paintings.

Vondelpark

A beautiful, sprawling public park in the heart of Amsterdam, perfect for a relaxing walk or picnic.

Dam Square

Amsterdam’s central square, home to the Royal Palace and the National Monument.

The Canal Ring

The UNESCO-listed canals are a hallmark of Amsterdam's unique architecture and history.
